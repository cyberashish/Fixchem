FC-11 (Gray) high polymer modified cement based tile adhesive, Suitable for fixing small wall and floor tiles in internal situation. Application in difficult and demanding situations consult FIXCHEM regarding use of a higher performance adhesive.

FEATURES - High polymer modified adhesive • Easy mixing & smooth application & better work ability • High strength • For use on walls and floors interior & exterior

High Polymer Modified Adhesive : FC-33 (Gray) features a high polymer modification, ensuring a robust bond for a wide range of tiles in different applications.

Easy Mixing & Smooth Application : The adhesive offers easy mixing, providing a smooth consistency for application. Its better workability allows for precise and hassle-free tile installations.

High Strength : With a focus on durability, FC-33 (Gray) provides high strength, ensuring a reliable and long-lasting bond for both walls and floors in both interior and exterior settings.

Versatile Usage : Ideal for a variety of tile types and sizes, this adhesive is suitable for walls, floors, and internal as well as external applications. It accommodates tiles of varying dimensions, including large-format tiles.

Packaging : Conveniently available in 20 kg packaging, making it suitable for projects of various scales.

Shelf Life & Storage : The shelf life of FC-33 (Gray) is 12 months from the date of manufacturing when stored in unopened conditions. Ensure storage in a cool and dry place for optimal performance.

Make your tile installations a breeze with Tile Adhesive - FC-33 (Gray). Trust in its high polymer modification, easy application, and versatility to bring efficiency and durability to your tiling projects.
