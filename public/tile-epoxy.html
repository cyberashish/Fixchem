Epoxy Grout is a cutting-edge three-part system, consisting of PART A, PART B, and PART C, designed for seamless application and optimal joint filling for tiles and stones. This epoxy resin-based grout offers a smooth, creamy consistency when mixed, ensuring a perfect paste for application. Its remarkable resistance to chemicals, abrasion, and high impact makes it an ideal choice for various applications. The cured grout forms a tough, impermeable layer that stands up against food spills, bacteria, and dirt, maintaining a clean and hygienic surface.

FEATURES - Polymer modified grout • Stain less and easy to clean, easy to mix and apply. • Provides excellent chemical resistant property. • Provides hygienic, hard wearing & impervious properties. • Non-cracking or powdering. • Non-Toxic & restricts entry of bacteria / dirt.

Three-Part System : Comprising PART A, PART B, and PART C for ease of application and optimal joint filling.

Smooth & Creamy Consistency : Mixes to a smooth, creamy paste for easy and precise application, ensuring a flawless finish.

Chemical Resistance : Exhibits excellent resistance to chemicals, enhancing its durability and suitability for diverse environments.

High Impact Resistance : With higher impact resistance, this epoxy grout is suitable for areas prone to heavy use and potential impact.

Stainless & Easy to Clean : Stain-resistant and easy to clean, maintaining a polished appearance even in high-traffic areas.

Hygienic & Impervious Properties : Provides hygienic, hard-wearing, and impervious properties, making it a top choice for areas where cleanliness is paramount.

Non-Cracking or Powdering : Ensures a durable and long-lasting finish without the risk of cracking or powdering over time.

Non-Toxic & Bacteria/Dirt Resistant : Non-toxic formulation restricts the entry of bacteria and dirt, contributing to a healthier and cleaner environment.

Elevate your surfaces with Epoxy Grout, a resilient and hygienic solution that offers unmatched durability and ease of maintenance.
