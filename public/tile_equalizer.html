Fixchem Tile Spacers serve a fundamental purpose by maintaining equal distances between tiles, ensuring a symmetrical and professional finish. However, their benefits extend beyond just spacing. They contribute to achieving a well-installed and aesthetically pleasing tile arrangement. The advantages of Tile Spacers include:

Symmetrical Results : IEnables precise spacing, ensuring tiles are evenly and symmetrically placed.

Professional Finish : Enhances the overall appearance of tile installations, contributing to a polished and professional finish.

Ease of Installation : Simplifies the tile installation process, making it more efficient and reducing the likelihood of errors.

Application Areas: Tile Spacers are versatile and suitable for various application areas, including walls, floors, interiors, exteriors, dry and wet areas, showers, and swimming pools. Incorporate Tile Spacers into your tile installations for a hassle-free and visually pleasing outcome, ensuring precision and professionalism in every project.

Method of Application : For home tile installations, using Tile Spacers is essential for achieving optimal results. These small cross-like or T-shaped pieces, typically made of plastic, ceramic, or wood, help maintain equal distances between tiles. Here's how to use them :

Lay 4 tiles on the work surface.
Place spacers at the corners of each tile.
Push the tiles together until they cannot go further.
Complete the tile installation.
Remove the spacers, ready to reuse for future projects.
